# 📊 Employee Health Analytics Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Employee Health Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end Microsoft Excel project developed to analyze employee wellness, productivity, and health indicators through an interactive dashboard.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow—from raw data cleaning and transformation to KPI development, business analysis, and dashboard design—using advanced Excel features.

This dashboard enables HR professionals and management teams to monitor workforce health, identify potential health risks, and make data-driven decisions to improve employee well-being and productivity.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw employee health data into meaningful business insights by answering questions such as:

- Which departments have the highest health risk?
- How productive are employees across departments?
- Does smoking influence employee wellness?
- Which employee groups require immediate attention?
- How are stress levels, sleep hours, and heart rate related to overall wellness?
